A FEW WORDS ABOUT ME

All my life I saw myself in an ordinary “stable” job, although since childhood I was attracted to art. I decided to act sensibly and tried myself in the field of IT. For half a year I got a new profession - an html layout designer. After a year, I wanted to move on. My creative nature won and I made a choice in favor of web design.

I liked the combination of digital art and programming, and I was immediately hooked. From that moment, my journey as a UX / UI designer began. I am now a multidisciplinary designer who focuses on telling my clients' stories visually, through enjoyable and meaningful experiences. I specialize in responsive websites and functional user interfaces.
